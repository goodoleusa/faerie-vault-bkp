# Crystallization

Crystallization is the most important process in the system — and the most misunderstood. It is NOT compression. Compression is lossy: it makes text shorter by discarding information. Crystallization is integrative: new knowledge is tested against everything already known, duplicates merge into richer statements, contradictions resolve, cross-references form. The file gets shorter but the knowledge gets *deeper*. Requires LLM inference; cannot be scripted.

## Flow

**In:** NECTAR narrative (validated findings, full history), scratch MEM blocks (session observations)
**Out:** Updated HONEY (denser, richer, same or fewer lines), updated agent cards (training outcomes integrated)

## Relationships

- [[nectar-narrative]] — primary source; tail-30 is the working input
- [[honey-seed]] — primary output; receives integrated knowledge
- [[agent-cards]] — secondary output; training outcomes crystallized here
- [[debloat-cycle]] — enforces budget; triggers crystallization when a file exceeds limit
- [[equilibrium]] — crystallization IS the equilibrium mechanism for memory

## Natural Metaphor

A supersaturated solution forming a crystal: the water (NECTAR) holds more knowledge than it can sustain indefinitely. When conditions are right, a crystal precipitates — perfect lattice structure, each atom in its optimal position. The water is depleted; the crystal is more than the sum of what went in.
