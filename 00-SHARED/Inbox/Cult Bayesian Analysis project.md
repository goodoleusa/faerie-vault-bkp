### Part 2: Bayesian Walkthrough – Grading Credibility & Updating Priors

Now that we have the sources, here is the step-by-step process to run the Bayesian analysis. This is how you convert "stories" into "data points" for your archive.

#### Step 1: Define the Hypotheses ($H$)

For each group, define the specific harm you are testing.

- _Example (Kingston):_ $H_{incest}$ = "The group practices systematic incest to maintain bloodlines."
- _Example (Twelve Tribes):_ $H_{abuse}$ = "The group uses excessive corporal punishment as a religious mandate."

#### Step 2: Establish the Prior ($P(H)$)

Before looking at the specific evidence for _this_ group, what is the baseline probability?

- _Logic:_ Based on historical data of similar groups (fundamentalist, isolationist, charismatic leader), what is the chance of abuse?
- _Assignment:_
    - **Low Prior (10%):** For groups with no prior legal history.
    - **Medium Prior (40%):** For groups with isolated allegations but no convictions.
    - **High Prior (80%):** For groups with a history of similar crimes (e.g., if a leader has a prior record).

#### Step 3: Grade the Evidence ($E$) – The Credibility Matrix

This is where you apply the "wide net" and grade every story. Assign a **Likelihood Ratio (LR)** to each piece of evidence.

- $LR = P(E|H) / P(E|\neg H)$
    - _$P(E|H)$_: How likely is this story if the group _is_ abusive?
    - _$P(E|\neg H)$_: How likely is this story if the group is _innocent_ (false accusation)?

**The Credibility Grading Scale:**

|Evidence Type|Description|Example Source|Estimated LR|Reasoning|
|---|---|---|---|---|
|**Tier 1: Judicial Fact**|Conviction, sworn affidavit, police report.|Jacob Kingston Plea, Tony Alamo Sentencing|**100+**|Extremely unlikely to be fabricated; legally binding.|
|**Tier 2: Corroborated Testimony**|Multiple independent survivors telling the same story + physical evidence.|CBC Doc (Ant Hill Kids), VICE (Kingston)|**20-50**|High consistency reduces the probability of coincidence.|
|**Tier 3: Single Witness**|One survivor account, no physical proof, but plausible.|Anonymous Reddit post, single interview|**5-10**|Plausible, but could be isolated or exaggerated.|
|**Tier 4: Rumor/Unverified**|Internet gossip, single anonymous post with no detail.|Unmoderated forum thread|**1-2**|Could be true, but also easily fabricated.|
|**Tier 5: Discredited**|Proven false or retracted.|Debunked conspiracy theory|**0.1**|Actually lowers the probability of the hypothesis.|

#### Step 4: Calculate the Posterior ($P(H|E)$)

Update your belief for each group.

- _Formula:_ $Odds_{posterior} = Odds_{prior} \times LR$
    
- _Example Calculation for Kingston Clan ($H_{fraud}$):_
    
    - **Prior:** 40% (Odds = 0.67)
    - **Evidence:** Jacob Kingston's guilty plea (Tier 1, LR = 100).
    - **Calculation:** $0.67 \times 100 = 67$ (Odds).
    - **Posterior Probability:** $67 / (67 + 1) \approx 98.5%$.
    - _Conclusion:_ The hypothesis is virtually certain.
- _Example Calculation for Twelve Tribes ($H_{abuse}$):_
    
    - **Prior:** 30% (Odds = 0.43)
    - **Evidence:** Multiple survivor stories (Tier 2, LR = 30) + One conviction (Tier 1, LR = 100).
    - **Combined LR:** $30 \times 100 = 3000$.
    - **Posterior:** $0.43 \times 3000 = 1290$ (Odds) $\rightarrow$ ~99.9%.
